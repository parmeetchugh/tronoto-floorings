Our 6 Step Renovation Process

What we do

We offer high quality full house renovation services to residents and business owners across the Greater Toronto Area. Here is a step by step guide of the process we use to achieve these breathtaking rewards.

01
Design, Selections + Pricing

During the free estimate and consultation, we look, listen, & learn what is important to you with regards to this project. Our team of designers can anticipate and solve problems unique to your space and formulate solutions that will place you within your budget.

02
Manage + Oversee Construction

Let our design and construction team supervise the renovation and construction. We make sure your home or office is treated with respect and the worksite is cleaned up daily.

03
Start demolition

Once all the materials and finishes are picked, it is time to start the project. We bring in all the materials, throw out all the garbage and remove any unwanted materials from the site. The framing will have to be the first step of construction.

04
Work behind the walls

Once the framing is complete it is time for plumbing, electrical, and HVAC to be installed. Then comes drywall, taping and sanding.

05
Add finishing touches

The last step is the finishing touches, such as paint, tiling, and fixture installation. Then comes the final inspection where all the materials, craftsmanship, and fixtures get tested and inspected, down to every screw and detail.
